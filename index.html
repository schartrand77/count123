Roadmap

From prototype to accounting platform

The goal is not another bookkeeping toy. Count123 should become the open source finance operating system teams can actually run a company on.

Phase 01

Trust the ledger

Build chart of accounts, journals, posting rules, and period close controls.

Phase 02

Own money movement

Ship AP, AR, invoices, bills, payments, and reconciliation workflows.

Phase 03

Automate reporting

Deliver statements, rollforwards, audit exports, and analytics APIs.

Phase 04

Scale across entities

Consolidation, intercompany eliminations, role controls, and integrations.
