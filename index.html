Roadmap

From prototype to practical bookkeeping tool

The goal is practical software for Canadian small businesses: balanced books, faster invoicing and purchasing workflows, cleaner reporting, and fewer spreadsheets.

Phase 01

Cover daily operations

Ship clients, quotes, invoices, purchase orders, bills, categories, and receipt capture.

Phase 02

Balance the books

Add reconciliation, trial balance, close workflows, and exception handling for book accuracy.

Phase 03

Handle Canadian tax

Add GST/HST tracking, remittance workflows, year-end exports, and accountant handoff packages.

Phase 04

Run the full back office

Add reminders, approvals, document workflows, and integrations for the core needs of a small business.
